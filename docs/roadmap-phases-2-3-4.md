## Phase 3: Complex Payroll System

**Objective:**
Automate payroll calculations and reporting, integrating with shift data and supporting various pay structures.

**Key Features:**
- Track employee hours, overtime, and shift differentials.
- Support for multiple pay rates, bonuses, and deductions.
- Generate payroll reports for admins and export to CSV/PDF.
- Integration with third-party payroll providers (optional, e.g., ADP, Gusto).
- Employee self-service access to pay stubs and history.
- Admin tools for payroll adjustments and approvals.

**Technical Considerations:**
- Extend database schema to support payroll data.
- Implement secure payroll processing logic in backend.
- Ensure compliance with local labor laws and tax regulations.
